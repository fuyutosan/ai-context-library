# Shelf: hobbies-creative

Personal creative work, hobby pieces, and projects made for fun live here.

## Writing tips
- Note *why* you're making it (because it's fun / as a gift / etc.) — it helps the AI assist with planning later
- Mark finished vs. unfinished
- Rough idea dumps and material notes are welcome (messy is fine)
